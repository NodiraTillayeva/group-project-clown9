About Us

Our company was established in 2021 in the frames of our Final Project from Internet Programming. This Project work made us think about solving a problem of poor homeless animals. On the Internet we daily see announcements about pets people want to give away. So we decided to connect people who want to give away animals and people who want to adopt one. We believe this will definitely help people realize their dreams of adopting a pet and will descrease amount of pets that can become homeless as their owners cannot take care of them anymore!
